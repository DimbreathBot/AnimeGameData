O Remus, player of the grand symphony, king of all the world.
Your music is heard across the heavens! It resounds throughout the earth.
When Sybilla's Golden Bee gives you a warning,
O Merciful One, grant us eternal life.
Proclaim this sacred moment,
That it should be praised for all eternity.

Copied from "The Third Hymn," Movement 5, Stanza 1.
