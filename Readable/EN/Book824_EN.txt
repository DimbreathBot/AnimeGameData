3. Rubble
An unknown period of time elapses before a group of mechanical puppets clear the rubble and reactivate the buried protagonist. They tell her that tens of thousands of years have passed, and the world is now ruled by mechanical puppets.
The first to lead mechanical puppets to resist were the companions rescued by the protagonist, thus making her a heroine to puppet-kind.
The mechanical puppets ask the protagonist what she wishes for, but she only wishes to stand on stage once more.
