Outline, by Scene

1. Ruins
The protagonist, a dancer, is abandoned in a ruin.
She suspects that she was abandoned by her companions because her legs had developed some sickness, causing her to no longer dance as smoothly as she once did.
It is not until she encounters a group of abandoned puppets identical to herself in the ruins that she realizes that this sickness is merely the aging of her components, and she is just a "dancing puppet."
