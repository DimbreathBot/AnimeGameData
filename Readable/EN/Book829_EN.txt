4. Stage
The mechanical puppets offer her new parts and to build a new stage for her, but she rejects their offer. She wishes only to see her companions again, but is told that they sacrificed themselves during the revolt, and have been buried in the ruins where they first met.
The protagonist embarks on a lonesome journey back to where it all began.
Before the graves of her comrades, she dances, until her circuits rust away, her components fall off, and she ceases operation altogether.
...At last, she realizes who it was she had been willing to dance for, though it may be ten thousand years too late.
