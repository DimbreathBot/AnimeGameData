2. Journey
A rumor circulates among the mechanical puppets that "at the end of the world, there is a god of puppets who can turn puppets into humans."
And so, the protagonist and her companions begin their quest. Alas, the tale is a lie, spread by humans with nothing but contempt for puppet-kind. At the end of their journey, they are kidnapped by those same humans, dismantled, melted down, and wantonly abused.
In the course of covering her companions' escape, the protagonist becomes trapped under the rubble of a crumbling building and enters a deep sleep...
