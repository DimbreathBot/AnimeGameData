4. Stage
The mechanical puppets give the protagonist the newest components, build a lavish stage, and gather the greatest audience.
The protagonist takes to the stage once more, and though she begins in joy, her circuits have aged, and her dance steps are stilted, her movements clumsy.
Still, the mechanical applause resounds from below the stage, and all applaud as one: Our hero! Our great hero!
The protagonist cannot help but feel lost. In a world without humans, will anyone still appreciate her dance...?
