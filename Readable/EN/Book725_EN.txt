He was once wrongly accused and, as he was about to be thrown into prison, saved by the selfless members of the press.
Thanks to the honorable ladies' arguments, Freeman was freed.
As his benefactor Euphrasie said:
"Truth is always marching onward, and nothing can stop it."
He has been continuing and waiting. Waiting for those who will carry on his ideals.

If you have read this far, friend, are you willing to believe in justice and hope?
If you share those ideals, please light a flame.
<color=#00E1FFFF>Of the four flames, only that which is set upon the land is the flame of hope. Light it to receive a token of Freeman's gratitude.</color>
